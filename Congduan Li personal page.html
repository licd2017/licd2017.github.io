Research

My research interests lie in information theory, network coding, source and channel coding, wireless communications, sensor networks, etc.

My current research topics include:
  1. Characterize the region of entropic vectors, from both outside (non-Shannon inequalities) and inside (matroid theory)
  2. Develope effective algorithms to use computers to proove rate regions for general multi-terminal networks
  3. Characterize the properties of networks for which simple linear codes suffice for the entire rate region

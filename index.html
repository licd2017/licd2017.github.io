Research

My research interests lie in information theory, network coding, network information security, wireless communications, distributed storage and caching systems, social networks, optimizations, machine learning, etc.

Most of my research projects require both mathmatics and  programmings.  Some of them are as follows.
  1. Characterize the region of entropic vectors, from both outside (non-Shannon inequalities) and inside (matroid theory)
  2. Develope effective algorithms to use computers to proove rate regions for general multi-terminal networks
  3. Characterize the properties of networks for which simple linear codes suffice for the entire rate regions of various netowrks
  4. Information source detection problem in spreadings of social networks
  5. Power contrained resource allocation in wireless networks
  6. Information theoreitical security for various networks
  7. Information theory in machine learning
